`PunctSide` is a feature of paired punctuation that are used before and after content in a sentence, i.e., parentheses and quotation marks.

### <a name="Ini">`Ini`</a>: Initial pair

#### Examples

* [myv] _«_ 
* [myv] _(_ 
* [myv] _[_ 

### <a name="Fin">`Fin`</a>: Final pair

#### Examples

* [myv] _»_
* [myv] _)_ 
* [myv] _]_
