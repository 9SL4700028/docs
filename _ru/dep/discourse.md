title: 'discourse'
shortdef: 'discourse element'

At present we use the `discourse` relation for redundant or emotional items, redundant coreferential pronoun.

~~~ sdparse
Ты же обещал ! \n You still promised !
discourse(обещал, же)
~~~
(meaning “… but you promised”)
