title: 'nmod'
shortdef: 'nominal modifier'
udver: '2'
---

The `nmod` relation is used for nominal modifiers that depend
on another noun. It corresponds to an attribute, or genitive complement (the terms are less standardized here).

~~~ sdparse
"PT no governo"
nmod(PT, governo)
~~~
