title: 'nsubj:pass'
shortdef: 'passive nominal subject'
---

`nsubj:pass` marks the subject of a passive clause.

~~~ sdparse
Das Bild wurde von Picasso gemalt . \n The painting was painted by Picasso .
nsubj:pass(gemalt, Bild)
~~~
