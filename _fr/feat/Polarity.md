In the four corpora **UD_French-ParTUT**, **UD_French-Sequoia**, **UD_French-PUD** and **UD_French-GSD**, the feature `Polarity=Neg` is used on adverbs (_ne_, _pas_), on determiners (_aucun_) or pronouns (_rien_).
