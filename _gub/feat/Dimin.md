`Dimin`  

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes 

Diminutive *a'i* is a noun modifier always bound to its host.

#### Examples

* _<b>Pixik-a'i a'e taw</b>&nbsp;_ “That village is very small” (small-DIM PRON village)
