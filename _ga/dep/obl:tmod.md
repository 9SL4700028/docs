This label is used when a nominal phrase has a temporal aspect and is acting adverbially, attaching to a verb.  

### Examples

_Uaisle saibhre de chuid na Róimhe a bhí ina magistri <b>tráth</b> a bhíodh ann. 'Rich Roman noble that was in charge <b>once</b>_

~~~ sdparse
Uaisle saibhre de chuid na Róimhe a bhí ina magistri tráth a bhíodh ann. \n Noble rich of the [] Rome REL that was in-his master
obl:tmod(bhí, tráth)
~~~ 

_Is é Micheal D. Higgins ba chionsiocair leis an Roinn a bhunú sa bhliain 1992. 'Michael D. Higgins was the contributor to the department in 1992_

~~~ sdparse
Is é Micheal D. Higgins ba chionsiocair leis an Roinn a bhunú sa bhliain 1992. \n 
obl:tmod(bhunú, bhliain)
~~~
