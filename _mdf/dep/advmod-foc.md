title: 'advmod:foc'
shortdef: 'advmod with focus modality'

---

The _advmod:foc_ relation is used for adverbal modifiers of focus.

~~~ sdparse
А мезе кда рамась , аньцек налхксемс , а мон ёран лиендемс . \n And what if he bought [one], only for play, but I want to fly around.
advmod:foc(налхксемс-7, аньцек-6)
advmod:foc(to_play-7, only-6)

~~~
