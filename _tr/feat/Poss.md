title: 'Poss'
shortdef: 'possessive'
---

We do not use `Poss` in Turkish.
Possessive use of a pronoun (similar to English _my_, _your_)
corresponds pronouns with _genitive_ [case](Case) suffix, e.g.,
_ben<b>im</b>_ "my".
