A [cc]() is the relation between a conjunct and a preceding `coordinating conjunction`.


~~~ sdparse
Писал е доста повести и драми .
cc(драми-6, и-5)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
Wrote_he is many stories and dramas .
cc(drammas-6, and-5)
~~~
