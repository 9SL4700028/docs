title: 'dobj'
shortdef: 'direct object'
---

A direct object is a nominal which is the (accusative) object of the verbal predicate.

~~~ sdparse
Vi skal ikke gjøre samme feil \n We are not going to make the same mistake
dobj(gjøre,feil)
~~~
