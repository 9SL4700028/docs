`VerbForm` is typically a feature of [verbs](pcm-pos/VERB). 

### <a name="Fin">`Fin`</a>: finite


#### Example

* _some Hausa people dey <b>get</b> am dere_ "some Hausa people get it there"



### <a name="Ger">`Ger`</a>: gerund

Gerund is a non-finite verb form that shares properties of verbs and nouns.


#### Example

* _how many minute wey dem do <b>overhauling</b> ?_ "For how many minutes are they overhauling ?"
