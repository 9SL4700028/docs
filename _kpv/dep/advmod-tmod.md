The _advmod:tmod_ relation is used for adverbal modifiers with temporal modality.

~~~ sdparse
Ваньӧ ӧдйӧ муысь чеччис . \n Vanjo quickly stood up from the ground .
advmod:tmod(чеччис, ӧдйӧ)

~~~
