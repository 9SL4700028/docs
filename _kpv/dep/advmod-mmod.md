title: 'advmod:mmod'
shortdef: 'advmod with locative modality'
udver: '2'
---

The _advmod:mmod_ relation is used for adverbal modal modifiers.

~~~ sdparse
Ӧд мунны колӧ бура унакодь на. \n As you know, there is still a good way to go.
advmod:mmod(колӧ, Ӧд)

~~~
