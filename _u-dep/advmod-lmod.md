A locative adverbial modifier is a subtype of the [advmod]() relation: if the modifier is specifying a location, it is labeled as `lmod`.

Danish:

~~~ sdparse
Han bøjer sig ned . \n He bends himself down .
advmod:lmod(bøjer, ned)
~~~
