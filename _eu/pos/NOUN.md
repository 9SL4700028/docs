### Definition

Nouns are a part of speech typically denoting a person, place, thing,
animal or idea.

### Examples

- _neska_ "girl"
- _katu_ "cat"
- _zuhaitz_ "tree"
- _aire_ "air"
- _edertasun_ "beauty"
