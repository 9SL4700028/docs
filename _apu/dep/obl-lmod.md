The _obl:lmod_ relation is used for obliques with locative modality.

~~~ sdparse
Nynuwa tiwitxi kai達apuku aapukutxi達 . \n They had a lot of things in the house .
obl:lmod(kai達apuku, aapukutxi達)

~~~
