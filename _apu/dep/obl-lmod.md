The _obl:lmod_ relation is used for obliques with locative modality.

~~~ sdparse
Nynuwa tiwitxi kaiãapuku aapukutxiã . \n They had a lot of things in the house .
obl:lmod(kaiãapuku, aapukutxiã)

~~~
