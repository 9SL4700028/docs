### Definition

Punctuation marks are character groups used to delimit linguistic units in printed text. 

Punctuation is not taken to include logograms such as _$_, _%_, and _§_, which are instead tagged as <a>`SYM`</a>.

### Examples

- Period: 。
- Comma: ， 、 
- Quotation marks: ‘ ’ “ ”「 」 『 』
- Title marks: 《 》
