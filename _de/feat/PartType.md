### <a name="Res">`Res`</a>: response particle

Called particles in the German grammar, they are tagged as [interjections](/u/pos/INTJ.html) following the UD guidelines.

#### Examples

* _<b>ja</b>_ “yes”
* _<b>nein</b>_ “no”
