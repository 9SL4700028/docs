acl:tmod is used for clausal modifier of noun of time (adnominal clause).
~~~ sdparse
Tôi ngủ trong khi họp .
obl:tmod(ngủ, khi)
acl:tmod(khi, họp)
compound(khi, trong)
nsubj(ngủ, Tôi)
punct(ngủ, 。)
~~~
