An adjectival modier of an NP is any adjectival phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the
NP.

~~~ sdparse
Nam ăn cá sống 。
amod(cá, sống)
det:pmod(Cô, ấy)
obj(ăn, cá)
nsubj(ăn, Nam)
punct(ăn, 。)
~~~
