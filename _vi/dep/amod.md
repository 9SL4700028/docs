title: 'amod'
shortdef: 'adjectival modifier'
---

An adjectival modier of an NP is any adjectival phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the
NP.

<pre><code class="language-sdparse">Nam ăn cá sống 。
amod(cá, sống)
det:pmod(Cô, ấy)
dobj(ăn, cá)
nsubj(ăn, Nam)
punct(ăn, 。)
</code></pre>
