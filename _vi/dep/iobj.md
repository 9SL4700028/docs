title: 'iobj'
shortdef: 'indirect object'
---

The indirect object of a VP is the noun phrase which is the object of the verb.

<pre><code class="language-sdparse">Hắn tặng cô ấy hoa 。
dobj(tặng, hoa)
iobj(tặng, cô)
nsubj(tặng, Hắn)
det:pmod(cô, ấy)
punct(tặng, 。)
</code></pre>
