Relation compound:prt is used for verb phrase and auxiliary/particle modifiers for verbs.

~~~ sdparse
Tôi để lại sách.
obj(để, sách)
compound:prt(để, lại)
nsubj(để, Tôi)
punct(để, 。)
~~~
