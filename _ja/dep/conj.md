title: 'conj'
shortdef: 'conjunct'
---

The dependency type `conj` represents coordination.
Following the definition of UD, the head of the dependency is the first conjunct.

~~~ sdparse
太郎 と 次郎 \n Taro and Jiro
cc(太郎, と)
conj(太郎, 次郎)
~~~
