udver: '2'

The specialization is used in PoSTWITA for paratactic sentences with an implicit nsubj role with respect to the governing predicate. 

~~~ sdparse
Mario Monti? Può salvare l'Italia 
parataxis:nsubj(salvare, Mario) 
~~~
