Boolean feature of nouns and adjectives. It signals that the word is an augmentative. This is one of the dimensions of evaluative modification, which encompasses different suffixes.

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

The augmentative is realized by appending *asú*, *usú* or *wasú* to nouns and adjectives.

#### Examples

Augmentative formations attested by Avila (2021)

* _<b>miráwasú</b>&nbsp;_ “big tree” (*mirá-wasú* tree-AUG)
* _<b>amanawasú</b>&nbsp;_ “heavy rain” (*amana-wasú* rain-AUG)
* _<b>yawaratewasú-itá</b>&nbsp;_ “big jaguars” (*yawarate-wasú-itá* jaguar-AUG-PL)
* _<b>yakwaimawasú</b>&nbsp;_ “very silly” (*yakwaima-wasú* silly-AUG)
