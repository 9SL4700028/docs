Boolean feature of nouns and adjectives. It signals that the word is a diminutive. This is one of the dimensions of evaluative modification, which encompasses different suffixes.  

### <a name="Yes">`Yes`</a>: Yes

The diminutive is realized by appending *i*, *í* or *mirĩ* to nouns and adjectives.

#### Examples

Diminutive formations attested by Avila (2021) or Navarro (2016):

* _<b>itaí</b>&nbsp;_ “little stone” (*ita-í* stone-DIM)
* _<b>yuraraí-itá</b>&nbsp;_ “little turtles” (*yurara-í-itá* turtle-DIM-PL)
* _<b>pirá-mirĩ-itá</b>&nbsp;_ “little fishes” (fish-DIM-PL)
* _<b>purangamirĩ</b>&nbsp;_ “beautiful” (*puranga-mirĩ* beautiful-DIM)
