`dep:cop` is used for the copular-like derivational suffix, ~:(ng)u(N→V) (to be N), for the morpheme-level annotations.

```
Qikmi-i-gu-q					.
qikmigh(N)-~:(ng)u(N→V)-[Ind.Intr]-[3Sg]	.
"It is a dog."
```

~~~ sdparse
qikmi i gu q . \n dog to-be-N Ind.Intr 3Sg .
dep:cop(qikmi, i)
dep:cop(dog, to-be-N)
~~~
