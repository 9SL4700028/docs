Yupik transitive verbs agree in person and number with their subject and object.

### <a name="Sing">`Sing`</a>: singular object

#### Examples

* [ess] _<b>Neghaqa</b> perara ellnginaan._ "<b>I ate</b> the meat aspic only."

### <a name="Dual">`Dual`</a>: dual object

#### Examples

* [ess] _Piyukuvek qergesek <b>qelpeghtikek</b>._ "If you like, open <b>the window</b>."

### <a name="Plur">`Plur`</a>: plural object

#### Examples

* [ess] _Pagunghaat <b>aavgii</b>._ "<b>She divided</b> the crowberries in two."
