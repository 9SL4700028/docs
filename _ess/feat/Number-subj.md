Yupik verbs agree in person and number with their subject.

### <a name="Sing">`Sing`</a>: singular subject

#### Examples

* [ess] _Pagunghaat <b>aavgii</b>._ "<b>She divided</b> the crowberries in two."

### <a name="Dual">`Dual`</a>: dual subject

#### Examples

* [ess] _Qakun <b>igleghlleqestung?</b>._ "When <b>will we take off?</b>."

### <a name="Plur">`Plur`</a>: plural subject

#### Examples

* [ess] _Qafsinaneng nanughmeng <b>esghaatetsi</b>?_ "How many polar bears <b>did you all see</b>?"
