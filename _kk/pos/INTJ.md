An interjection is a word that is used most often as an exclamation or
part of an exclamation.

#### Examples

* [kk] _жоқ_, "no", _иә_ "yes
* [kk] _сәлем_, "hello"
* [kk] _әлбетте_, "of course"
* [kk] _аһ_
