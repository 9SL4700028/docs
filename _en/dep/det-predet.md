---
layout: relation
title:  'det:predet'
shortdef : 'predeterminer'
---

A predeterminer is the relation between the head of an NP and a word
that precedes and modifies the meaning of the NP determiner.

~~~ sdparse
All the boys are here
det:predet(boys, All)
~~~
