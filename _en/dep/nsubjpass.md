---

title:  'nsubjpass'
shortdef : 'passive nominal subject'
---

A passive nominal subject is a noun phrase which is the syntactic
subject of a passive clause.

~~~ sdparse
Dole was defeated by Clinton
nsubjpass(defeated, Dole)
~~~
