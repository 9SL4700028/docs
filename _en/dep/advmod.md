An adverbial modifier of a word is a (non-clausal) adverb or adverbial
phrase (ADVP) that serves to modify the meaning of the word.

~~~ sdparse
Genetically modified food
advmod(modified, Genetically)
~~~

~~~ sdparse
less often
advmod(often, less)
~~~
