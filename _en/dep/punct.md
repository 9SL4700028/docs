layout: relation
title:  'punct'
shortdef : 'punctuation'
---

This is used for any piece of punctuation in a clause, if punctuation is being retained in the typed dependencies. By default, punctuation is not retained in the output. 

~~~ sdparse
Go home !
punct(Go, !)
~~~
