title: 'amod'
shortdef: 'adjectival modifier'
---

An adjectival modifier of an NP is any adjectival phrase that serves to modify the meaning of the NP.

~~~ sdparse
Encuentran materiales peligrosos .
amod(materiales, peligrosos)
~~~
