title: 'obl'

The `obl` relation is used for non-core nominal dependents of clausal predicates. 

~~~ sdparse
PRO1(B) FÖDD SKÖVDE@en \n I was born in Skövde
obl(FÖDD, SKÖVDE@en)
~~~
