The `advmod:neg` relation is used for the negative items *ma(n)* and *na*.

~~~sdparse
Man kewaʼ taj ri alajibʼ .
advmod:neg(kewaʼ, Man)
aux(kewaʼ, taj)
det(alajibʼ, ri)
nsubj(kewaʼ, alajibʼ)
~~~
