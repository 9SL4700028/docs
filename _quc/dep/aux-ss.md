A status suffix echos the tense, aspect, movement and transitivity of 
the clause that it is dependent on.

~~~ sdparse
Konojel ri alaxik keʼetzʼan- -ik .
aux:ss(keʼetzʼan-, -ik)
nsubj(keʼetzʼan-, Konojel)
det(alaxik, ri)
nmod(Konojel, alaxik)
~~~
