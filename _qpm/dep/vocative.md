`vocative` (vocative): The vocative relation is used to mark a dialogue participant addressed in a text.

~~~ sdparse

  Ahmet,    what   exists   in   the other world?       
           Ahmét,     kakná íma      na    drúgono  düņó?   
              |___vocative__|
                     ←
~~~
