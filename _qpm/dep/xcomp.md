`xcomp` (open clausal complement of a verb or an adjective): A predicative or clausal complement without its own subject.
~~~ sdparse
                         the two sisters started  to  whisper   softly
                         dvéte    séstry zafátiho da šáptot so  usúlna                                                             
                                             |___xcomp__|
                                                   →
~~~
